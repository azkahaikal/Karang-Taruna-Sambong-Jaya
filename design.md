# Design Style for Karang Taruna Sambong Jaya Website

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Forest Green (#2D5016) - representing growth, community, and Indonesian nature
- **Secondary**: Warm Terracotta (#C65D07) - symbolizing energy, youth, and local earth tones
- **Accent**: Golden Yellow (#F4A261) - highlighting achievements and optimism
- **Neutral**: Warm Cream (#F7F3E9) - providing clean, readable backgrounds

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for headings and hero text
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - clean, modern sans-serif for readability
- **Accent Font**: "Poppins" - friendly sans-serif for buttons and highlights

### Visual Language
- **Community-Centered**: Design emphasizes togetherness and local pride
- **Youthful Energy**: Dynamic layouts with engaging interactions
- **Cultural Respect**: Incorporating Indonesian design elements subtly
- **Professional Trust**: Clean, organized structure for credibility

## Visual Effects

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: For smooth page transitions and element animations
- **Typed.js**: For dynamic text effects in hero sections
- **Splide.js**: For image carousels and galleries
- **ECharts.js**: For program statistics and impact visualization
- **p5.js**: For creative background effects and community visualizations

### Effect Implementation
- **Header Background**: Subtle particle system representing community connections
- **Text Animations**: Typewriter effect for hero headlines with color cycling
- **Image Effects**: Ken Burns pan/zoom on hero images
- **Scroll Animations**: Gentle fade-in effects for content sections
- **Hover Interactions**: 3D tilt effects on cards and buttons

### Styling Approach
- **Layout**: Grid-based design with generous white space
- **Imagery**: High-quality photos showcasing community activities
- **Icons**: Custom SVG icons representing youth organization values
- **Shadows**: Soft, natural shadows for depth without harshness
- **Borders**: Subtle rounded corners for modern, friendly appearance

### Header Effect
- **Background**: Animated gradient flow in forest green and terracotta
- **Particles**: Floating dots representing community members connecting
- **Typography**: Large, bold headings with gradient text effects
- **Navigation**: Clean, minimal design with smooth hover transitions

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for smartphone usage in rural areas
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large buttons and easy navigation
- **Performance**: Optimized images and efficient animations
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and keyboard navigation
